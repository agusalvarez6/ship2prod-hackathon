# Judging criteria

| Category | Weight | What judges look for |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| **Idea** | 20% | Does the solution have the potential to solve a meaningful problem or demonstrate real-world value? |
| **Technical implementation** | 20% | How well was the solution implemented? |
| **Tool use** | 20% | Did the solution effectively use at least 3 sponsor tools? |
| **Presentation (demo)** | 20% | Demonstration of the solution in 3 minutes. |
| **Autonomy** | 20% | How well does the agent act on real-time data without manual intervention? |
